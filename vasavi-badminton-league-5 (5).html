The Organiser

A community court, five editions strong.

Vasavi Badminton League is organised by the Manjunathnagar Vasavi Yuvajana Sangha (R.) — bringing the Arya Vysya community together for a day of competitive badminton, blessings, and belonging.

Manjunathnagar Vasavi Yuvajana Sangha (R.) has run the Vasavi Badminton League for five editions running, growing it into one of the community's most anticipated annual fixtures — open to Arya Vysya members aged 18 to 60 from across South India.

Every match day begins with the blessings of Goddess Vasavi, and every category is built to bring out fair, spirited competition — single-elimination, professionally officiated, and entirely community-run.

Fair Play

General instructions & guidelines.

Open only to Arya Vysya community members, aged 18–60.

Indoor synthetic court — non-marking shoes are compulsory.

Li-Ning Future 10 shuttlecocks will be used throughout.

Breakfast, lunch & high-tea provided on match day.

Participation certificates for every entrant.

Last date to register: 5th August 2026.

Outstation accommodation available on request, before 5th Aug.

Minimum 16 registrations required for a category to run.

No refunds once payment is received.

Each player may enter a maximum of 2 categories.

Ties are held 3 days prior, at Manjunathanagara Vasavi Temple.

Single-elimination format — win and advance.

Professional or seeded players may not participate — and will be disqualified if found.

Organisers' & umpires' decisions are final.
